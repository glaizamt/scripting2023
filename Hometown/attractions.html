Cebu Travel

Another significant economic driver in Cebu is tourism. Cebu is a wonderful place to visit. In Cebu, there are several white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and marine life. History buffs will find plenty of historical places in Cebu to satiate their cravings. One can experience some incredible locations in Cebu. Due to its accessibility to the city and the variety of its natural scenery, South Cebu has emerged as one of the top tourist destinations in the nation. Southern Cebu is made up of a number of municipalities, the most well-known of which are Oslob, Dalaguete, Badian, and Moalboal. To be honest, southern Cebu is still one of my favourite places to visit. Travelling south should be on the itinerary; there will be trekking, waterfall chasing, sardine swimming, island hopping, and even the possibility to get up close and personal with a whale shark, or "butanding" in Cebuano.
