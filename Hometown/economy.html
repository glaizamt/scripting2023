About

Ceboom, a combination of Cebu and Boom!

With the greatest expansion, Cebu City emerged as the hub of commercial and economic activity in the Visayas-Mindanao area. The fast rise of the manufacturing sector, which includes sectors like electronics, food processing, and furniture manufacturing, has been a major contributor to Cebu's remarkable economic success. There appears to be a growing number of businesspeople settling in Cebu, and the city's expanding economy is supported by the business process outsourcing (BPO), tourism, information technology (IT), creative sectors, and a skilled labor force. The majority of real estate firms in Cebu have their roots in Manila, which is one of the factors fostering the city's business growth. Cebu's economy has benefited from this, which is why the country has maintained its stellar record for years.
